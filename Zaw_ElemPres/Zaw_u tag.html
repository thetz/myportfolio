The < u > tag is used for some text that should be stylistically different from normal text, such as misspelled words and Chinese proper names such as the names of people, places and organizations.

It is easy to mispell the word "misspell".

我 來 自 美 國.

I am from America. with America is written in Mandarin ( traditional ) and marked as a proper name.

< u > tag supports global attributes and event attributes in HTML.
